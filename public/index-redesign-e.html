FAQ

Questions worth answering.

How much does it cost?

+

Coaches set their own rates — typically $49 to $199/mo. Everything's included: programming, messaging, video check-ins, nutrition.

Can I cancel any time?

+

Month-to-month. Cancel from your dashboard.

How are coaches vetted?

+

Every coach is reviewed for credentials, insurance, and a sample program before going live.

How fast do coaches get paid?

+

Within 48 hours of the subscription charge, via Stripe Connect.
