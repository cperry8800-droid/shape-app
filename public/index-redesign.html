FAQ

Questions worth answering.

Not a compliance wall. If something's missing, email us.

How much does it cost?

+

Coaches set their own subscription rates — typically $49 to $199 a month. Everything is included: programming, messaging, video check-ins, nutrition. No a-la-carte add-ons.

Can I cancel any time?

+

Month-to-month, no annual lock. Cancel from your dashboard; no email chains, no exit interview.

How are coaches vetted?

+

Every coach is reviewed for credentials, insurance, and a sample program before going live. Client ratings are surfaced publicly and we remove coaches who can't sustain a 4.5+ average over 50 sessions.

How fast do coaches get paid?

+

Payouts land within 48 hours of the subscription charge, direct to the coach's bank via Stripe Connect. No reserve periods.

Is there a community component?

+

Yes. Every subscription includes access to Shape's client community, coach-hosted challenges, and Shape Radio — playlist programming your coach curates to match your sessions.
