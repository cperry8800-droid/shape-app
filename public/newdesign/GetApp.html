Everything Shape, in one app

Scroll the tour.

Each tab does one thing well. Together they're the whole loop — train, eat, plan, recover, share.

01HOME

Today, at a glance.

The Shape Daily — your day, top to bottom. Calendar, calorie ledger, what's left to log, who's on the air, day brief from your coach. Skim it in five seconds, dive in if you want.

02TRAIN

The session, written by your coach.

Coach-programmed sessions land on your phone the night before. Log sets in two taps. Bar speed and RPE flow back to your trainer for next-week's plan.

02WORKOUT PREVIEW

Know what's coming.

The card view — every move, every set, every cue from your trainer — before you walk into the gym. Tap a move for video. Note your warmups, mark a substitute.

03EAT

Daily meal plans, macros tallied live.

Cook-along playlists from your nutritionist, swaps for travel days, templates for fast-week chaos. The day's calorie deficit (or surplus) ticks as you log.

03EAT — STEP-BY-STEP

Cook the recipe, no scroll war.

Recipes break down into one step at a time on screen. Hands greasy? Tap to advance. Ingredients map back into your grocery list automatically.

04GROCERY LIST

Auto-built from the week. Sent to Instacart in a tap.

Organized by aisle with a live running cost. Build and save your own custom lists, save your nutritionist's lists straight to your Library. Coach swaps land inline — "swap salmon for cod if the price jumps." One tap to Send to Instacart.

05RECIPES

A recipe a day, your nutritionist's library on tap.

Filter by prep time, macros, or dietary preference. Save your own. Tap any recipe to add its ingredients to your grocery list.

06HABITS

Do's, don'ts, streaks.

Tap to log. Earn Shape Score for every check. Pick friends or other followers to be notified when you miss — accountability loop without the guilt trip.

07SHAPE SCORE

Five tiers. Monthly points. Real rewards.

Raw, Tempo, Form, Peak, Legend. Earn for workouts, plan adherence, habits, PRs, community kudos. Cash them in at the Shape Store.

08RADIO

Music that locks to your heart rate.

Live mixes curated by your coach. Each track's BPM syncs with your wearable (Apple Watch, Whoop, Garmin) — when the two lock in, your pace becomes the song. Reactive visualizer reads the audio, not a stock animation.

09FEED

The social layer, opt-in.

PRs, workouts, runs, tier-ups, streaks, posts — Strava-style with a community board baked in. Drop a kudos, reply, share to a friend. Public to the community, scoped to your team, or private. You pick.

10CHAT

Letters & replies.

One-tap messaging with your trainer, nutritionist, and training partners. Drop into the Shape Community Chat for borough-level run clubs, lift partners, race-day groups, AMAs with coaches. No app switching, no inbox archaeology.

11PERSONALIZE

Light paper, dark paper, cream, ink.

Pick the colors of your background, accents, and tab bar — the "Shape Daily" home skin is one of several. Match the app to your phone's wallpaper, your gym, or your mood.

Why an app

Real coaches.
Real plans.
Pocket-sized.

Shape isn't a chatbot. Every program is written by a vetted trainer or nutritionist who reads your logs, asks how you're feeling, and adjusts each week. The app keeps that loop tight — daily.

Your data stays yours. Wearables sync. Coaches see what you let them.

Common questions.

When does the app launch?

+

iOS first — TestFlight beta this quarter, App Store launch shortly after. Android follows. Drop your email below and we'll send the link the day it ships.

Is it free?

+

The app is included with any Shape membership ($5/mo). Sessions with coaches are billed separately at their listed rate.

What if I already use the website?

+

Your account, coaches, plans, history, and Shape Score all carry over. Sign in to the app with the same email — the data shows up.

Does it work with Apple Health, Whoop, Garmin?

+

Yes. HealthKit on iOS, plus first-party integrations with Whoop, Garmin, and Strava. Heart rate, sleep, and workout data flow into the app and back to your coach.

Will my coach reach me through the app?

+

Direct messages, weekly check-ins, and timestamped reactions on your workouts and meals — all in-app. Push notifications opt-in, of course.
