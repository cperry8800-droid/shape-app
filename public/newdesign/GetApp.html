Everything Shape, in one app.

01

Train

Coach-programmed sessions land on your phone the night before. Log sets in two taps. Bar speed and RPE flow back to your trainer for next-week's plan.

02

Eat

Daily meal plans and macro tracking. Cook-along playlists from your nutritionist, swaps for travel days, templates for fast-week chaos.

03

Grocery list

Your week's meals roll up into one shopping list. Build and save your own custom lists. Save your nutritionist's lists straight to your library. Integrated with Instacart — one tap to checkout. Updates automatically when your nutritionist swaps a meal.

04

Recipes

Recipe of the day, plus a library of your nutritionist's picks. Filter by prep time, macros, or dietary preference. Save your own. Tap any recipe to add its ingredients to your grocery list.

05

Habits

Do's, don'ts, streaks. Tap to log. Earn Shape Score for every check. Pick friends or other followers to be notified when you miss — accountability loop without the guilt trip.

06

Score

Five tiers — Raw, Tempo, Form, Peak, Legend. Earn monthly points for workouts, plan adherence, habits, PRs, community kudos. Cash them in at the Shape Store.

07

Radio

Live music for training, curated by your coach. Each track's BPM syncs with your live heart rate (Apple Watch, Whoop, Garmin) — when the two lock in, your pace becomes the song. Reactive visualizer reads the audio, not a stock animation. Heart the good ones, send to friends.

08

Feed

The social layer. PRs, workouts, runs, tier-ups, streaks, posts — everything people training alongside you are up to. Strava-style with a community board baked in. Drop a kudos, reply, share to a friend. Public to the community, scoped to your team, or private. You pick.

09

Chat

One-tap messaging with your trainer, nutritionist, and training partners. Drop into the Shape Community Chat to stay in touch with the entire Shape community — borough-level run clubs, lift partners, race-day groups, AMAs with coaches. No app switching, no inbox archaeology.

10

Personalize

Light paper, dark paper, cream, ink. Pick the colors of your background, accents, and tab bar — the "Shape Daily" home skin is one of several. Match the app to your phone's wallpaper, your gym, or your mood.

Why an app

Real coaches.
Real plans.
Pocket-sized.

Shape isn't a chatbot. Every program is written by a vetted trainer or nutritionist who reads your logs, asks how you're feeling, and adjusts each week. The app keeps that loop tight — daily.

Your data stays yours. Wearables sync. Coaches see what you let them.

Common questions.

When does the app launch?

+

iOS first — TestFlight beta this quarter, App Store launch shortly after. Android follows. Drop your email below and we'll send the link the day it ships.

Is it free?

+

The app is included with any Shape membership ($5/mo). Sessions with coaches are billed separately at their listed rate.

What if I already use the website?

+

Your account, coaches, plans, history, and Shape Score all carry over. Sign in to the app with the same email — the data shows up.

Does it work with Apple Health, Whoop, Garmin?

+

Yes. HealthKit on iOS, plus first-party integrations with Whoop, Garmin, and Strava. Heart rate, sleep, and workout data flow into the app and back to your coach.

Will my coach reach me through the app?

+

Direct messages, weekly check-ins, and timestamped reactions on your workouts and meals — all in-app. Push notifications opt-in, of course.
