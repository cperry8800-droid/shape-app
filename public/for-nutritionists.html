Who Can Join

Built for credentialed nutrition professionals

Shape accepts a range of qualified nutrition experts. All practitioners are reviewed before listing.

Registered Dietitian

RD / RDN — Licensed by the Commission on Dietetic Registration. The gold standard in medical nutrition therapy.

Certified Nutrition Specialist

CNS — Advanced credential for nutrition professionals with a graduate degree and supervised practice hours.

Certified Nutritionist

CN — State-licensed nutritionist with comprehensive training in diet planning and client education.

Health & Nutrition Coach

Certified coaches specializing in behavior change, meal planning, and long-term habit formation.

Registered Dietitians

Beyond meal plans — the full scope of an RD

Shape supports the complete breadth of Registered Dietitian practice. If your license covers it, Shape gives you the tools to deliver it.

  • Medical nutrition therapy for chronic conditions — diabetes, kidney disease, GI disorders
  • Eating disorder support and intuitive eating frameworks
  • Sports nutrition and performance fueling
  • Clinical nutrition assessments and nutrition diagnoses
  • Evidence-based counseling for cancer, heart disease, and post-surgical recovery
  • Prenatal, pediatric, and geriatric nutrition
